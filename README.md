# Employee Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## 1. Problem Statement

Employee attrition can affect an organization's productivity and growth. This project analyzes employee data to understand workforce trends, employee satisfaction, and factors that may contribute to attrition.

---

## 2. Dataset Description

The dataset contains employee information such as:

* Employee ID
* Age
* Gender
* Department
* Job Role
* Monthly Income
* Attrition Status
* Overtime
* Job Satisfaction
* Environment Satisfaction
* Work-Life Balance
* Performance Rating
* Years at Company

---

## 3. KPI Definitions

### Total Employees

Total number of employees in the organization.

### Attrition Count

Number of employees who left the company.

### Attrition Rate

Percentage of employees who left the company.

### Average Job Satisfaction

Average employee satisfaction score.

### Average Environment Satisfaction

Average workplace environment satisfaction score.

### Average Work-Life Balance

Average work-life balance score.

### Average Performance Rating

Average employee performance score.

## 5. Key Findings

* Employee distribution is balanced across departments.
* Overtime employees show higher attrition risk.
* Job satisfaction and work-life balance impact employee retention.
* Business travel may influence attrition.
* Some job roles experience higher attrition than others.

---

## 6. Recommendations

* Improve work-life balance programs.
* Reduce excessive overtime.
* Increase employee engagement and recognition.
* Provide career growth opportunities.
* Focus retention efforts on high-risk employee groups.

---

## 7. Future Scope

* Add predictive attrition analysis using Machine Learning.
* Create employee risk scoring models.
* Integrate real-time HR data.
* Build advanced workforce forecasting dashboards.
